About APOD

Each day a different image or photograph of our fascinating universe is featured, along with a brief explanation written by a professional astronomer. The Astronomy Picture of the Day (APOD) is based upon work supported by NASA under award No. 80NSSC17M0076. Any opinions, findings, and conclusions or recommendations expressed in this material are those of the author(s) and do not necessarily reflect the views of the National Aeronautics and Space Administration.
